___________ Homemade strawberry ice-cream! ___________

🔸🔸🔸

Nothing better than a refreshing dessert such as this one!

~*🍦 🍧 🍨*~

Ingridients:

  • Strawberries
  • Milk and cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla and salt
  • Food coloring (optional)

Description:

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl.

    2. 🔸

  2. Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker; freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.

    3. 🔸

  3. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm.

⤴

Should look like this

🔸

Store your strawberry ice cream in an airtight storage container, recycled ice cream container,
or zip-top freezer bag. Try to enjoy the ice cream within a few weeks for the best taste.
