🔸🔸🔸

The most comforting soup made from bone broth and vegetables, no like, serously trust me, you'll end up feeling like this:

Ingridients:

  • Chicken legs
  • Two large onions
  • A large carrot
  • Fresh parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Description:

  1. Start by putting the chicken legs into a large cooking pot and prepare a grater and a small knife aside.

    2. 🔸

  2. Prepare your carrot by washing, peeling and grating it into the pot, peel the onions and put them as well and put a generous amount of parsley.

    3. 🔸

  3. Add your seasonings and two table spoons worth of olive oil.

    4. 🔸

  4. As a last step fill your pot with water, put a lid on and set to medium heat and cook for minimum of 2 hours.

⤴

Should look like this

🔸

You can optionally shred the chicken into the soup or prepare another meal from it.
When storing, make sure you take the onions out and put the soup in the fridge for maximum of three days.
